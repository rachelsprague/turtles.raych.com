# Guppies

My planted tanks are home to communities of **guppies**, small livebearing fish known for their colors and constant activity.

The guppies live in planted aquariums where plants, fish, and microorganisms create a balanced ecosystem.

---

## About the Guppies

- Species: *Poecilia reticulata*
- Behavior: Active, social fish that constantly explore the tank
- Reproduction: Livebearers — new generations appear regularly
- Habitat: Planted aquariums with natural hiding places
