## Setup

- **Tank Size:** 20 gallons  
- **Substrate:** Play sand
- **Lighting:** NICREW ClassicLED aquarium light
- **Heating:** EHEIM 3614 heater
- **Filtration:** Sponge filter with Aquarium Co-Op powerhead
- **Air:** Adjustable air stone powered by a shared **Tetra 20–40 gallon air pump** with an air manifold.

## Plants & Decorations

- **Floating plants:** Duckweed  
- **Submerged plants:** Anubias, Cryptocoryne Wendtii ‘Red’, Java Moss
- **Hardscape:** Driftwood

## Inhabitants

- **[Guppies](https://turtles.raych.com/animals/guppies/)** 
- **Tank Mates:** Ramshorn snails
- **Feeding:** Aquarium Co-op Easy Fry Food

## Notes & History

- Established: 2022
- Water change schedule: 50% bi-weekly  
- All current residents originated from Guppy Tank #1.
