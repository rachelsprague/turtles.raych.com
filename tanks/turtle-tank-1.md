## Setup

- **Tank Size:** 75 gallons  
- **Substrate:** River rocks + sand mix  
- **Lighting:** UVB + basking lamp  
- **Heating:** Water heater (78–80°F), basking area (~90°F)  
- **Filtration:** Canister filter with bio-media  

## Plants & Decorations

- Floating plants: Water lettuce, duckweed  
- Submerged plants: Java fern, Anubias  
- Basking platform: Driftwood + sloped rocks  
- Hiding spots: PVC caves and half logs  

## Inhabitants

- **Turtles:** 3 Red-Eared Sliders (~20 years old)  
- **Tank Mates:** None (turtles only)  
- **Feeding:** Pellets, leafy greens, occasional protein treats  

## Notes & History

- Established: 2008  
- Water change schedule: 30% weekly  
- Special observations: Likes basking near the sunset; most active at mid-day  
- Future improvements: Consider adding additional floating plants for shade
